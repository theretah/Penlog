Penlog - Read about it
Penlog is an online platform where people can write about topic they know, and read about topic they may not know.

-Authentication-
Users can sign up to follow their favorite authors, like or save their favorite post, or comment where they have 
a question. An anonymous user can only read posts and visit profiles.

-Posts-
A signed in user can post about any topic, which can be made from at most 3 categories. A post can be liked, saved,
be commented on, or reported of not following guidelines.

-Users-
A signed in user can visit their profile page, where all their posts are contained. Along with the number of users
they are follow or are being followed by them. They also can see posts they have liked or saved.

-Categories-
A signed in user should recieve recommendations based on his interesting topics.
